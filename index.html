About Best Pancakes

Situated in Haddington, East Lothian, Best Pancakes offers the most delicious take-out pancakes you can find. We listen to our customers and do encourage everyone to tell us their favourite toppings. Based on popularity, those toppings make their way into our menu. For those who cannot pick between toppings, they can custom build their pancakes with the toppings we have available.
